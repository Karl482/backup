About Us

Welcome to Karlito's Kapehan sa Gabi, your go-to spot for relaxation and good vibes in Brgy. Marinig, Cabuyao City. Nestled in the heart of Celestine Homes, we offer a cozy and laid-back atmosphere where you can unwind after a long day or catch up with friends over a delicious cup of coffee. We’re open every evening from 5:00 PM to 2:00 AM, serving up a variety of expertly crafted coffees, teas, and tasty treats. Whether you're here to take a break, get some work done, or simply enjoy a peaceful moment, Karlito's Kapehan sa Gabi is the perfect place to do just that. Come for the coffee, stay for the comfort. We look forward to making your evenings a little bit brighter and a lot more relaxing.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To serve high-quality, affordable beverages and food in a warm, welcoming evening space that brings the community together, delivered with friendly service and consistent craftsmanship.

Vision

To be the neighborhood’s favorite late‑night coffee destination. A cozy, sustainable, and inspiring place where people connect, create, and relax.
